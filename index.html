Follow tutorial: Homebrewing Wii
+Todo lo que necesitas esta en el descripción del video.

At 16:19, before you start the USB Loader GX plug in your USB. You want the Wii to know that that is where you want your games to be downloaded y donde estan tus juegos. Plug in your USB and click on the USB Loader GX and do not do anything else until it moves past the code and stuff. Once you are in the new Wii menu he ends up going to settings to change something. When he goes to change the "MOUNT USB at launch" you do not have to change anything! Leave it as is.

Stop watching at 23:30!!!!


+Look at the right side of the page and look for a place that says "Links". Click "Download OSC (Recommended)". From here a zip folder should be downloaded. Extract the files and open it. Now look for the folder that says "homebrew_browser" Copy it and go back to your sd card. Go to apps and delete The HomeBrew Browser file you already have in there. Once deleted, paste the homebrew_browser file you just copied here.

So a run down of what you will do:
+You will need to go back into your computer and input the sd card and open the app folder in the sd card. Delete the file you have for the HomeBrew Browser and paste the new "homebrew_browser" file you just copied/obtained into the app folder in your sd card.


+Once downloaded and the zip folder is extracted, search for "wiimmfipatcher" folder and copy and paste onto app folder on sd card. Slip sd card into wii and go to The Homebrew channel and search for the Wiimmfi channel on there. Load it and it should start downloading some stuff and then it will ask you to insert a game. Insert a game and once inserted the game will start playing as normal. Go back to the homebrew channel screen. Now run the HomeBrew Browser. Once run you are set!

Tu USB sigue deberia estar en tu Wii. Si no esta en tu Wii you've missed a step but if you do have it do the following. Vete a USB LOADER GX. When USB LOADER GX loads tus juegos deberian estar ahí, en tu pantalla! Si estan ahí preciona uno de los juegos y chequella si puedes jugar online! You should be able to play online but dejame saber si estas teniedo un problema!
